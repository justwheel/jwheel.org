* * *

_one, two_  
I fell in love with you  
Caught me by surprise

_three, four_  
My focus was on you  
There was me, you, & the road

_five, six_  
I took each breath  
Felt each beat of my heart

_seven, eight_  
I realized Your rhythm  
Pulled into Your pace

_nine, ten_  
I became my breath  
Centered at Your feet

_one, two_
