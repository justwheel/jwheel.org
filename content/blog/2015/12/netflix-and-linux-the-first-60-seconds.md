## Netflix, Linux, and 60 seconds

While they have their differences on the desktop, there is one place where Netflix and Linux get along beautifully: the cloud. Netflix system administrator [Brendan Gregg](https://plus.google.com/112610724469645265130) recently published an article on the [Netflix blog](http://techblog.netflix.com/2015/11/linux-performance-analysis-in-60s.html) about what their administrators do in the first 60 seconds when analyzing performance. You should [give it a read](http://techblog.netflix.com/2015/11/linux-performance-analysis-in-60s.html)!
