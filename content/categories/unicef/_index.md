This category covers my contributions, interests, and experiences with the United Nations Children's Fund, [UNICEF](https://www.unicef.org/). This includes Open Source endeavors at the UNICEF [Office of Innovation](https://www.unicef.org/innovation/), the [Ventures Team](https://www.unicef.org/innovation/ventures), the [UNICEF Innovation Fund](https://www.unicefinnovationfund.org/), the [Digital Public Goods Alliance](https://digitalpublicgoods.net/about/), [Giga Connect](https://gigaconnect.org/), and more.
