UNICEF Innovation

I worked on the MagicBox project with the UNICEF Office of Innovation from January - May 2018. MagicBox is a geospatial data project connecting unrelated datasets into actionable feedback for local governments and municipalities. I improved open source best practices to enable agile development and created an open source community implementation plan.
